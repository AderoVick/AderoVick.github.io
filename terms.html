Service terms

Clear expectations before work begins.

Last updated: July 2026. This is a practical starter template, not a substitute for legal advice.

Quotations and scope

Website estimates are indicative. Work begins only after the scope, deliverables, timeline, price and payment schedule are confirmed in writing.

Client responsibilities

The client must provide accurate instructions, lawful access to the submitted data, timely feedback and any required institutional approvals. The client remains responsible for final decisions made using analytical outputs.

Payments

Deposits, milestone payments or full payment may be requested depending on the project. Payment instructions must match an approved quotation. Provider fees, currency conversion and blockchain network fees may apply.

Revisions

The quotation should state the number and type of revisions included. New requirements or material scope changes may require a revised price and timeline.

Confidentiality

Reasonable efforts will be made to protect non-public client information. Highly sensitive or regulated data should not be submitted until appropriate security and data-processing arrangements are agreed.

Intellectual property

Ownership and reuse rights should be specified in the quotation. Unless otherwise agreed, third-party software, open-source libraries and pre-existing reusable methods remain subject to their original licences.

Acceptable use

The service may not be used for unlawful activity, academic dishonesty, impersonation, fraudulent documentation, unauthorised surveillance or infringement of third-party rights.

Cancellation and refunds

Cancellation terms depend on work completed, non-refundable provider fees and committed project time. Any refund should be documented and returned through an appropriate method.
