Privacy notice

How client information is handled.

Last updated: July 2026. This template should be reviewed before production launch and adapted to the actual hosting, analytics and payment providers used.

Information collected

The platform may collect contact details, organisation information, project briefs, deadlines, quotations, order-status records, payment references and files voluntarily submitted for a service request.

Purpose

Information is used to review requests, prepare quotations, provide services, communicate progress, process approved payments, prevent abuse and maintain business records.

Payments

Payment providers process payment credentials on their own secure services. The platform should store only transaction references, amounts, currencies and payment status—not card numbers, PINs, recovery phrases or wallet private keys.

Files

Client files should be stored in a private bucket with access controls. Avoid submitting credentials or unnecessary sensitive personal information. Files should be retained only as long as needed for the agreed service and legal recordkeeping.

Sharing

Information may be shared with contracted infrastructure and payment providers only as needed to operate the service. It should not be sold to advertisers.

Your choices

You may request access, correction or deletion of eligible records by emailing bornfacevictor@gmail.com. Some transaction and contractual records may need to be retained.

Security

Reasonable technical controls are used, but no internet service can guarantee absolute security. Report suspected issues through the contact address rather than posting them publicly.
